E-recharge, short for electronic recharge, is a convenient and digital method of adding credit to your mobile phone or other devices. It allows users to top up their phone numbers or accounts using online platforms or mobile apps.

Using e-recharge is simple. Visit our platform or mobile app, by registering an account and making a deposit choose the "Recharge" option, enter the required details such as the phone number and desired credit amount, and complete the transaction. Your phone will be topped up instantly.

Recharge credit is the monetary value added to your mobile phone or account through the e-recharge process. It enables you to make calls, send messages, and use mobile services based on the credited amount.

To recharge your phone number, log in to our platform or mobile app. Navigate to the "Recharge & Bill Payment" section, enter your phone number, choose the desired credit amount, and proceed to make the payment. Your phone will be recharged promptly.

Absolutely. Our e-recharge service supports various mobile operators, allowing you to recharge phone numbers across different networks. Simply choose the operator when entering the phone number during the recharge process.

Yes, for added convenience, we offer an auto-recharge option. You can set up automatic recharges at regular intervals, ensuring your phone always has sufficient credit without the need for manual intervention.

It's essential to double-check the phone number before confirming the e-recharge. If you enter the wrong number, the credit will be applied to that number, and we cannot reverse the transaction. Please ensure accuracy to avoid any inconvenience.
